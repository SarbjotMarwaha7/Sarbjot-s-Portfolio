About

Here is a summary of my journey.

Carpenter

As a carpenter, I’ve always had a passion for creating things with my hands—turning raw materials into something functional, beautiful, and meaningful. The process of measuring, cutting, and assembling pieces to bring a vision to life requires patience, precision, and a deep understanding of the tools and materials at hand. Whether it’s crafting furniture or designing intricate woodwork, I’ve learned that every creation starts with a solid plan, evolves with adaptability, and finishes with a sense of pride in what’s been built.

Software Engineer

Transitioning into coding feels like a natural extension of this creative journey. Just like in carpentry, coding starts with an idea and requires careful planning, problem-solving, and attention to detail. Instead of wood, the materials are lines of code; instead of a saw and hammer, the tools are IDEs and frameworks. Both crafts demand creativity, adaptability, and the ability to troubleshoot when things don’t go as planned. Ultimately, both carpentry and coding allow me to build something from nothing, solve real-world problems, and create experiences that others can use and appreciate.

Comfort Food
